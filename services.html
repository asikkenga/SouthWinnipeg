 ACUPUNCTURE

 Acupuncture is another treatment technique offered at our clinic, which can be included as part of your appointment. We use acupuncture to affect the muscle and nerve systems of the body which can help improve pain, reduce muscle tension, and improve circulation. We also offer dry needling at our South Winnipeg and St. Norbert Physiotherapy locations, which is a form of acupuncture that can be used to help release trigger points.

 ORTHOTICS

 SUFFERING FROM A CHRONIC CONDITION?

  Physiotherapy can also help those who live with chronic conditions/illness. Physiotherapists use an approach that considers the whole person including neurological, cardiovascular, and respiratory conditions. Visiting a physiotherapist can prolong your healthy years and help you achieve your physical activity goals.
